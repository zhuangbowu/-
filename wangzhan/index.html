公司简介

Company Profile

互联网打破了信息传播的地域和国家界限，信息自由和数字货币的发展将进一步打破基于国家界限的金融和价值传播障碍。其去中心化的理念和区块链技术的发展吸引了众多投资人的加入，Multivector希望抓住金融科技发展的机遇，成为中国数字货币投资研究的先行者，提供专业的数字货币投资理财服务。

The Internet has broken the boundaries of geographical and national information dissemination, and the development of freedom of information and digital currency will further break the barriers of financial and value dissemination based on national boundaries. The concept of decentralization and the development of blockchain technology have attracted many investors. Multivector is hoping to seize the key opportunities for the development of science and technology, become a pioneer of China digital currency investment market and provide professional digital currency investment services.
